Early Stage Scoliosis Detection

In scoliosis diagnosis detection, currently the treatment for scoliosis is based on spinal assessment manual study which has some limitations like it is very tedious, time-consuming and cost effective. Scoliosis diagnosis is a critical task in initial stages based on patient history records or on captured patient X-ray spine images.
